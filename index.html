How Rare Are You?

This site will allow for you to assess how rare you are in comparison to other people.
It uses global census statistics and multiplies the % of people in which have that, for standarized things e.g.(hair color, eye color, race.)
For things that are variable, such as IQ, Height, Size.
It follow simple formulas to deduce of which quantile you are from, then multiplying all values, to find out what % of people have the traits you do.
All this in conjunction allows you to determine How Rare You Are!

Common traits are as follows:

  • Height
  • IQ
  • Size
  • Hair Color
  • Eye Color
  • Race
